Logo Concept

Envelope with Rising Sun

The Glimt logo combines an envelope (representing connection and communication) with a vibrant orange sun (symbolizing warmth, discovery, and local travel experiences). The envelope's clean geometric lines embody professionalism and trust, while the sun's rays suggest guidance and enlightenment through local storytelling.

The bistre envelope provides grounding, while the flame-orange sun creates an energetic focal point, making it instantly recognizable and deeply connected to the brand's mission of bringing travelers and local experts together.
